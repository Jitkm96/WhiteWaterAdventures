Mochhu

The Mochhu River, also known as the Mo Chhu or the Mother River, flows through the beautiful Punakha Valley in Bhutan. Rafting here is a captivating experience that combines the adrenaline rush of white water with breathtaking views of lush green valleys and ancient temples. It's also a thrilling adventure amidst stunning natural landscapes.

Trip Details
  • Overall trip duration: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Rafting Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes on the water
  • Necessary rafting equipment provided (helmets, life jackets, and paddles)
  • Safety briefings and instructions by Guide.
  • Basic Rafting Training is provided by the guide
