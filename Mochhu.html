White Water Adventures

Mo Chhu

The Mochhu River, also known as the Mo Chhu or the Mother River, flows through the beautiful Punakha Valley in Bhutan. Rafting here is a captivating experience that combines the adrenaline rush of white water with breathtaking views of lush green valleys and ancient temples.Its also a thrilling adventure amidst stunning natural landscapes.

Trip Details
  • Rafting Duration: 1 hours 30 minutes on the water
  • Safety briefings and instructionon's by Guide.
  • Necessary rafting equipment (helmets, life jackets and paddles)
  • Basic Rafting Training is provided by te guide
