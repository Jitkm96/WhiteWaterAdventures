About Us

 

White Water Rafting Adventure, based in Punakha, Bhutan. offers thrilling rafting trips on the Phochhu and Mochhu Rivers.

Known for their commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental sustainability, they provide unforgettable experiences amidst breathtaking landscapes.

As one of the top rafting companies in Punakha Dzongkhag, they employ experienced guides trained in river safety, first aid, and rafting techniques. These guides lead groups through rapids, ensuring a safe and exhilarating adventure for all ages.
