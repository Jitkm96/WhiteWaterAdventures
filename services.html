Mid-route Rest and Menu

We provide a short rest during the route where you can relax amidst beautiful scenery. A small hut serves fresh items, making your break pleasant and enjoyable.

Locals Specialties

Experience the essence of Bhutanese cuisine with our local specialties. Enjoy fluffy steamed rice paired with Ema Datshi, a spicy dish made with chili peppers and cheese. Savor Kewa Datshi, featuring sliced potatoes cooked with chili and cheese, and indulge in tender Pork Pa and succulent Chicken Curry. Our steam veg selection offers a light and healthy accompaniment to these flavorful dishes.

Regional Favorites

Discover a variety of regional favorites with our diverse menu. Relish steamed rice alongside Sukha Roti, a traditional Indian flatbread, and Alu Sabji, a spiced potato curry. Enjoy Paneer Gravy and Chicken Gravy, both cooked in rich, creamy sauces. Our selection also includes Dal, a nutritious lentil dish, and Kewa Datshi for a comforting traditional meal.

International Dishes

Our international menu features a variety of dishes to cater to all tastes. Enjoy steamed rice and stir-fried noodles with a mix of vegetables and sauces. Delight in Beef Sliced Fried, Chicken Chilli, and steamed asparagus. We also offer a traditional Bhutanese item and a choice of hearty soup or nutritious dal, ensuring a satisfying meal for everyone.
