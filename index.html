Rafting Instructions and Safety Guidelines

Instructions:

  • 1. Wear comfortable clothing suitable for water activities; avoid cotton as it retains water and keeps you cold.
  • 2. Bring a change of clothes and a towel for after the rafting trip.
  • 3. Wear water-resistant sunscreen and secure any loose items, such as sunglasses, with straps.
  • 4. Do not bring valuables; leave them in a secure place or at home.
  • 5. Ensure you are in good physical condition and notify us of any medical conditions.
  • 6. Arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled trip for a briefing and to get geared up.

Safety Guidelines:

  • 1. Always wear appropriate rafting gear.
  • 2. Stay seated and hold on to the safety lines when going through rapids.
  • 3. Do not panic if you fall out; float on your back with feet downstream.
  • 4. Use the rescue rope if thrown to you, and listen to the guide’s instructions.
  • 5. Avoid standing up in moving water to prevent foot entrapment.
  • 6. Inform the guide if you have any medical conditions or concerns.
