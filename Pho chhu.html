Pho chhu

Pho Chhu(Male River) is one of the major rivers of Bhutans which tracks its source to Gasa District on the borders between Bhutan and Tibet. It joins with the Mo Chhu(Female River) at the confluence below punakha Dzong, the winter residence of Dratshang Lhentshog.
